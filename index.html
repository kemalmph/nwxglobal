Who We Are

A New World
Exchange

NWX Global is a dynamic, boundary-defying company headquartered in Bali and Jakarta, Indonesia — two of Southeast Asia's most vibrant epicenters — with a mandate to serve a global clientele without limitation.

We were founded on a singular conviction: that today's international business owners, high-achieving professionals, and digital nomads deserve a partner that understands both the complexity of global commerce and the human need for balance and wellbeing.

Through our three interconnected pillars — Ecosystem, Trading, and Investment — we provide the infrastructure, intelligence, and community that enables our clients to operate with confidence anywhere in the world.
